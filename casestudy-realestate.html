About the Client

A growing real estate agency spending too much time chasing leads manually.

The client is a mid-sized real estate agency based in Dubai with a team of 6 agents. They were generating leads through referrals and manual outreach — a slow, inconsistent process that left agents spending more time on prospecting than closing deals.

🏢

Real Estate Agency — Dubai, UAE

6 agents · Previously manual outreach only · 20–30 leads/month before automation · Goal: build a scalable, automated lead generation system that fills the pipeline without additional headcount.

Problem

Agents were spending 60% of their time on admin, not closing.

Every lead required manual research, a manually written outreach email, and individual follow-up tracking in a shared spreadsheet. There was no CRM pipeline, no automated follow-up sequences, and no way to scale outreach without hiring more staff.

Leads were falling through the cracks. Follow-ups were inconsistent. The agency was leaving money on the table every single week.

Solution

A three-layer automation stack: find, contact, and nurture — automatically.

Axythra built a full lead generation pipeline connecting Apollo.io for prospect discovery, Zapier for workflow orchestration, and GoHighLevel as the CRM and follow-up engine. The system finds qualified leads daily, enriches their data, adds them to a drip sequence, and moves them through the pipeline based on their responses — with zero manual input from the team.

Agents now only touch leads that have already expressed interest. Everything upstream is handled automatically.

Key Features

From prospect to pipeline — fully automated.

  • Apollo.io scrapes and filters qualified real estate investor leads daily based on custom criteria
  • Zapier automatically pushes new leads into GoHighLevel CRM with full contact details
  • AI-personalized outreach emails sent automatically within minutes of lead entry

Challenges & Problem-Solving

Personalization at scale — without it feeling robotic.

The Challenge

Generic outreach emails had extremely low reply rates

Initial test sequences using templated emails achieved less than 2% reply rates. Leads could tell the emails were automated. The system was working technically, but not converting.

Root Cause

No personalization layer in the outreach copy

Every lead was receiving the exact same email with only their first name swapped. For high-value real estate prospects, this level of personalization wasn't enough to stand out in a crowded inbox.

Resolution

AI-powered personalization node added to the Zapier workflow

An AI step was added to the Zapier workflow that pulls the lead's company, recent activity, and location from Apollo, then generates a personalized opening line for each email. Reply rates increased from under 2% to over 8% within two weeks of the change.

Outcome

A predictable, scalable lead machine — running 24/7.

The agency went from 20–30 manual leads per month to 200+ automated contacts per month, with agents only spending time on leads that had already shown interest. Pipeline revenue increased significantly in the first quarter after launch.
